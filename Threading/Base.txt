
A thread in this system is used to increase the effcienty of concurrent task as they are designed around the business logic as in 
orderintake and not. Examples in a trading system:
• Networking thread → receive raw messages
• Parsing thread → decode and validate orders
• Matching engine thread → update order book, match trades
• Market data thread → publish updates to clients

Each thread runs multiple functions internally, but they all belong to a single business task.
